About Open Day

Open Day is an annual event at IISc where the institute opens its doors to the public. It is a day of fun and learning for everyone. Team Vicharaka will be showcasing our rover and our work at the Open Day 2023. We will be conducting various demonstrations to engage with the public and inspire the next generation of scientists and engineers.
